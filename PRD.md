# 1. Executive Summary

The AI-Powered Placement Management Platform is a centralized web-based system designed to streamline and automate campus placement activities for students, recruiters, placement officers, and administrators.

The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence to improve job matching, automate candidate shortlisting, identify student skill gaps, and provide actionable placement insights. The objective is to increase placement success rates while reducing manual effort and improving communication among all stakeholders.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Current placement processes rely heavily on spreadsheets, emails, and manual coordination. This leads to:

* Inefficient student-job matching
* Time-consuming resume screening
* Lack of centralized placement tracking
* Delayed communication
* Difficulty generating placement reports
* Limited visibility into student readiness

An intelligent placement management system is required to automate these processes and improve overall efficiency.
